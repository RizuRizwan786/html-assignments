Environment Essay in 100 words
An environment is the natural surroundings which help life to grow, nourish and destroy on this planet called earth. Natural environment plays a great role in the existence of life

The environment refers to the natural world and all the living and non-living things that exist within it. This includes the air, water, soil, and all the plants and animals that live in these habitats.
